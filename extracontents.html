Dana's Diary
Dana tries to decode the old diaries of her mother, to help Karan from inside the Dome. The Gov finds out that Dana helped Karan escape and they try to imprison her. She manage to get away and the only safe place to escape to is the Outside World. Dana and Anhzo leave the Dome and try to find Karan. On their way they meet Atlas, and convince him of the wickedness of the Gov, so that he agrees to help them find Karan.
