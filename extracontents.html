Dana's Diary
Atlas finds Karan and Anhzo but he is angry for his mother’s death. He refuses to have anything to do with them. Atlas meets the Agents that IO sent Outside to find Karan. He helps them, because he’s mad at Karan. Atlas meets Dana and Anhzo.They convince him not to be mad at Karan, but instead to help him.
Atlas's Profile
Dana tries to decode the old diaries of her mother, to understand how she can help Karan from inside the Dome. The Gov finds out that Dana helped Karan escape the Dome, so they try to imprison her. She manage to get away and the only safe place to escape to is the Outside World. Dana and Anhzo leave the Dome and try to find Karan. On their way they meet Atlas, and they convince him of the wickedness of the Gov, so that he agrees to help them find Karan.
Anhzo's Blog
Anhzo goes back into the Dome with the help of Dana. They try to decode her mother’s diaries together.He decides to escape with Dana despite everything and to avoid being imprisoned.
