DIVE DEEP BRINGS YOU CLOSER TO SDG:
LIFE BELOW WATER
Dive Deep is a transmedia experience that seeks, through the creation of a fictional storyworld, to make people aware of the effects of our everyday behaviors on marine and coastal ecosystems and teach them small daily actions that can have a great positive impact if there is a community commitment. We look for people to understand that it's possible for all species to thrive as long as each species understands and respects its limits, seeks a balance and remains empathetic towards the other species.
